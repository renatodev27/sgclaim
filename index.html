About us

As Founder and President Brisia San German has dedicated her time and effort to help clients determine the proper values of Insurance Based Claims. As an adjuster I have developed a comprehensive understanding of the complexities and intricacies involved in determining accurate claim amounts.

Underestimating or overestimating the value of a claim can have significant financial implications for both the insured and the insurer. As an experienced professional, I believe that every claim should be evaluated with the utmost precision and attention to detail, ensuring fairness to all parties involved.

At SG Claims Adjusting Services we utilize proven methodologies and industry knowledge to determine the most accurate values for insurance claims. We understand that each claim is unique, and our tailored approach enables us to consider all relevant factors, including property damage, loss of income, personal injuries, and any additional expenses incurred as a result of the incident.

What is a Public Insurance Adjuster?

An insurance public adjuster is like a detective for your insurance claim. They investigate the damages, assess the value of your losses, and help you navigate the complex insurance process. They work on your behalf to maximize your claim settlement..

My insurance company said that my claim is denied!
What can I do?

Contact us today for a FREE claim review. We will review your complete claim situation with no obligation and we will discuss your individual claim situation with you to see if there is anything that we can do to help you receive a fair settlement for your claim. If we are unable to help we will let you know and will guide you as much as possible to help your situation.

3 Reasons Why You Should Hire A Public Insurance Adjuster
